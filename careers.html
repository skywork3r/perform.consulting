perform.consulting is a career accelerator for ambitious engineers who want serious growth.

We develop software engineers through mentorship, real project work, technical training, interview preparation, and U.S. market strategy. The goal is not to look strong on paper. The goal is to become strong enough to perform in demanding technology environments.

We are selective. We look for engineers with strong fundamentals, ownership, discipline, honesty, communication skills, and the ability to learn fast under real pressure.

To be considered, send your resume and a short note to grigory@perform.consulting.

Software Engineer - Accelerator Track

We are looking for highly motivated software engineers who want to grow into serious technical roles. The ideal candidate has strong computer science fundamentals, backend or full-stack ability, clear communication, and the willingness to take ownership of hard problems.

You should be comfortable working across backend, front-end, infrastructure, and product logic. Experience with scalable systems, APIs, cloud infrastructure, distributed systems, C#, Java, Go, Python, C++, React, or Flutter is valuable.

More important than any single stack: you must be able to learn deeply, think clearly, take feedback seriously, and bring problems to full resolution.

Requirements:

  • Strong computer science and software engineering fundamentals
  • Ability to build scalable, secure, and maintainable applications
  • Backend or full-stack development experience
  • Understanding of APIs, databases, distributed systems, and production software
  • Experience with C#, Java, Go, Python, C++, React, Flutter, or similar technologies
  • Clear communication and ability to work with engineers, product managers, and clients
  • Ownership mindset, discipline, and willingness to improve fast
  • Serious interest in long-term career growth

Responsibilities:

  • Build production-quality software across backend, front-end, infrastructure, mobile, or AI systems
  • Participate in architecture discussions, code reviews, and technical planning
  • Work on real projects with high technical standards
  • Learn the business and product context behind the systems you build
  • Strengthen your interview skills, communication, and career strategy
  • Follow strong engineering, security, and reliability practices
  • Continually improve your technical depth and professional judgment
